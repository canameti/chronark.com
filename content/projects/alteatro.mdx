Alteatro is a premium ice cream manufacturer with a rich tradition in crafting artisanal ice cream. The website was designed to reflect the elegance and quality of their products while providing an intuitive user experience.

## Key Features

- Modern, responsive design that adapts seamlessly across all devices
- Elegant product showcase highlighting their artisanal ice cream varieties
- Clean, minimalist interface that puts the focus on the product imagery
- Performance-optimized for fast loading times
- Built with Next.js and modern web technologies

The design philosophy centered around creating a digital presence that matches the premium quality of Alteatro's ice cream products, while ensuring easy navigation and accessibility for all users.
